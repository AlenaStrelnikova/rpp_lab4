In the quiet of the night,

Stars are shining, oh so bright.

Whispers of the gentle breeze,

Bring my heart a sense of peace.

Moonlight dances on the sea,

Painting dreams so wild and free.

Nature sings a lullaby,

As the world begins to sigh.

Close your eyes and drift away,

Into night, where shadows play.

In this moment, let it be,

Find your calm, just you and me.
